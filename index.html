XeniX Mobile Labs was founded by Sonsie Brown, a certified phlebotomist with multi-state and international clinical experience, during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic — when access to healthcare became a matter of life and death.

Witnessing firsthand how transportation barriers, fear of infection, and collapsing rural healthcare infrastructure kept people from critical diagnostics, Sonsie built XeniX to be the solution: a fully mobile, fully compliant, community-first diagnostic service that meets patients exactly where they are.

Founder spotlight — leadership in care, innovation, and patient access.

From individual patients in their homes to corporate wellness programs, nursing homes, and international COVID-19 response efforts, XeniX has consistently delivered accurate, compassionate, professional care — with revenue from day one and a compliance infrastructure that rivals established labs.

Today, XeniX operates across multiple states and continues to grow, driven by a simple belief: quality healthcare should come to you.
